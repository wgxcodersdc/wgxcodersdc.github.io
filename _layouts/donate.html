Women and Gender eXpansive Coders DC

Empowering women and non-binary technologists in the DC area

Our Mission

Women and Gender eXpansive Coders DC envisions a community where women and non-binary members feel empowered to express their learnings, share their experiences, and feel safe in bringing their unique visions to events. We seek to foster growth in technical skills, career opportunities, and friendship through virtual and in-person meetings.
